Sisig Recipe

Sisig is a sizzling Filipino dish made from chopped pork, seasoned with onions, chili, and calamansi, known for its tangy, savory, and slightly spicy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or pork face (ears, cheeks, snout), boiled and chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 upto 3 chili peppers, chopped
  • 3 upto 4 tablespoons calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar/optional
  • 1 egg
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • butter or oil for sizzling

How it's made (In Order)

  1. Boil the pork until tender about 30 to 45 minutes. Drain and let it cool.
  2. Grill or broil the pork until slightly crispy and charred for extra flavor.
  3. Chop the pork into small fine pieces.
  4. Saute onions in a pan with a little oil or butter until soft.
  5. Add the chopped pork and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Season with soy sauce, calamansi juice, vinegar (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  7. Add chili peppers and mayonnaise(if using). Stir thoroughly.
  8. Transfer to a sizzling plate and crack an egg on top
  9. Serve hot mixing the egg in before eating.
