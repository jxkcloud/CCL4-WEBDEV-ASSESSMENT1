Plot Overview

Kim Dokja is an ordinary office worker whose only hobby is reading a web novel called Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse. One day, the story suddenly becomes reality, and everything around him changes into dangerous scenarios where survival is the only rule.

Since Dokja is the only person who finished reading the novel, he knows what happens next, but even with that knowledge things dont always go exactly how he expected.

Key Information

  • Genre: Action / Fantasy / Survival
  • Original Authors: singNsong
  • Illustration: Sleepy-C
  • Platform: WEBTOON
  • First Release: 2020

Main Characters

Kim Dokja

The main protagonist who understands the world because he already knows the original story.

Yoo Joonghyuk

The original protagonist of the novel Dokja read. Very strong but difficult to trust.

Han Sooyoung

A clever writer with unpredictable decisions, often causing tension inside the group.

Reader Reviews

Many readers like how the story mixes survival, strategy and psychological conflict.

A lot of people also mention that the character development feels stronger as the story continues, even if some early chapters can be a little slow.
